Methodology

Entries are added manually from primary sources (official statements, wire coverage, org press releases) — not scraped or auto-generated, to avoid amplifying rumors. Each entry shows three custody stages: announced (a public pledge exists), operational (an organization is confirmed active on the ground or funds are moving), and confirmed delivered (independent reporting or audited data shows the aid reached affected people). Most pledges made in the first week sit at stage one or two — that gap is the point of this ledger, not a flaw in it.

Before giving directly

Cross-check any organization against a charity evaluator such as BBB's Give.org, Charity Navigator, or GiveWell before donating, and be wary of unsolicited social-media or messaging-app requests — scam activity typically rises after major disasters.
